> **"Docendo discimus"** (By teaching, we learn.)

For me, the journey of teaching is far more than just a transfer of knowledge; it is the cornerstone of both personal and academic growth. I believe that knowledge deepens as it is shared, and true mastery lies in the ability to distill complex concepts into their simplest forms. As part of my search for meaning, this process continuously reshapes my own learning path within every course and with every student.

### Teaching Assistantship (TA)

During my tenure as a **Research and Teaching Assistant** at **Yildiz Technical University** (2022–2025), I supported several core courses and conducted various laboratory sessions within the Department of Control and Automation Engineering.

* **System Dynamics, Modeling and Simulation**
* **Process Control**
* **Signals and Systems**
* **Real-Time Control Systems**
